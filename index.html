Asthma

  • About: A chronic condition where airways narrow and swell.
  • Symptoms: Shortness of breath, wheezing, chest tightness.
  • Do's: Use inhalers, avoid allergens.
  • Don'ts: Avoid smoke, cold air, and dust.
  • Precautions: Regular checkups, monitor breathing.
  • Concern: Frequent asthma attacks or difficulty breathing.

Anemia

  • About: Low red blood cells or hemoglobin in blood.
  • Symptoms: Fatigue, pale skin, dizziness.
  • Do's: Eat iron-rich foods, take supplements.
  • Don'ts: Avoid tea/coffee with meals.
  • Precautions: Regular blood tests, iron intake.
  • Concern: Persistent tiredness or fainting.

Allergies

  • About: Immune reaction to harmless substances like pollen or dust.
  • Symptoms: Sneezing, itching, rashes.
  • Do's: Take antihistamines, avoid triggers.
  • Don'ts: Avoid self-medication.
  • Precautions: Allergy testing, clean environment.
  • Concern: Severe reactions or breathing difficulty.

Acne

  • About: Skin condition causing pimples and blackheads.
  • Symptoms: Red bumps, pus-filled pimples, oily skin.
  • Do's: Wash face twice daily, use prescribed creams.
  • Don'ts: Don‚Äôt pop pimples or use harsh scrubs.
  • Precautions: Maintain skin hygiene, avoid oily cosmetics.
  • Concern: Severe acne or scarring.

Arthritis

  • About: Joint inflammation causing pain and stiffness.
  • Symptoms: Swelling, pain, limited movement.
  • Do's: Gentle exercises, anti-inflammatory meds.
  • Don'ts: Avoid inactivity and heavy lifting.
  • Precautions: Healthy weight, joint care.
  • Concern: Persistent joint pain/swelling.
