Obesity

  • About: Excessive body fat increasing health risks.
  • Symptoms: Breathlessness, fatigue, joint pain.
  • Do's: Follow diet, increase physical activity.
  • Don'ts: Avoid junk food and sedentary lifestyle.
  • Precautions: Regular exercise, portion control.
  • Concern: BMI above 30 or health issues.

Malaria

  • About: Mosquito-borne disease caused by parasites.
  • Symptoms: Fever, chills, sweating, headache.
  • Do's: Complete antimalarial treatment.
  • Don'ts: Avoid exposure to mosquitoes at night.
  • Precautions: Use nets, repellents, and mosquito control.
  • Concern: High fever with chills or recurring symptoms.

Measles

  • About: A highly contagious viral infection.
  • Symptoms: High fever, cough, red eyes, rash.
  • Do's: Isolate, take vitamin A supplements.
  • Don'ts: Avoid public places.
  • Precautions: MMR vaccination.
  • Concern: Breathing difficulty or ear infection.

Migraine

  • About: Severe headache often accompanied by nausea and sensitivity to light.
  • Symptoms: Pulsating headache, nausea, visual aura.
  • Do's: Rest in dark room, take migraine medication.
  • Don'ts: Avoid triggers like caffeine, stress.
  • Precautions: Keep regular sleep and meal schedule.
  • Concern: Frequent or worsening headaches.

Mumps

  • About: Viral infection that affects the salivary glands.
  • Symptoms: Swollen cheeks, fever, muscle aches, headache.
