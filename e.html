Heat Stroke

  • About: Severe condition caused by overheating of the body.
  • Symptoms: High body temperature, confusion, no sweating, fainting.
  • Do's: Move to a cool place, hydrate, use cold compresses.
  • Don'ts: Avoid sun exposure and dehydration.
  • Precautions: Wear light clothes, drink water regularly.
  • Concern: Any signs of confusion, fever above 104¬∞F.

Indigestion (Acidity)

  • About: Discomfort in the stomach after eating, often due to acid buildup.
  • Symptoms: Bloating, heartburn, nausea.
  • Do's: Eat small meals, chew food properly, drink water.
  • Don'ts: Avoid spicy, oily, or very fast eating.
  • Precautions: Eat on time, avoid lying down right after meals.
  • Concern: Persistent acidity, vomiting, or chest pain.

Influenza

  • About: Common viral infection affecting respiratory system.
  • Symptoms: Fever, chills, cough, body aches.
  • Do's: Rest, stay hydrated, take flu meds if prescribed.
  • Don'ts: Avoid crowded areas while infected.
  • Precautions: Annual flu vaccine, hand hygiene.
  • Concern: Severe symptoms or difficulty breathing.

Jaundice

  • About: Yellowing of skin/eyes due to liver dysfunction.
  • Symptoms: Yellow skin, fatigue, dark urine.
  • Do's: Eat light, take liver medications.
  • Don'ts: Avoid alcohol, oily food.
  • Precautions: Safe water, avoid hepatitis infection.
  • Concern: Severe fatigue, vomiting, or confusion.

Insomnia

  • About: Difficulty falling or staying asleep.
  • Symptoms: Sleeplessness, fatigue, irritability, poor focus.
  • Do's: Keep a sleep routine, limit caffeine, relax before bed.
  • Don'ts: Avoid screens before sleep, naps late in day.
  • Precautions: Good sleep hygiene, stress management.
  • Concern: If poor sleep affects daily life consistently.
